About Me

Leaving university in 2008, I had a Bachelors in Science (Biochemistry) qualification which I used, at first to go into teaching. To do so I spent 1 year passing my PGCE. From here I taught around Staffordshire and Shropshire over a period of 10 years.

During the pandemic of 2020, I took the opportunity to reflect and decided that I would follow a hobby of mine and learn to code to business standards. I enrolled in a course with Code Institute, choosing them as the bootcamp they offered gave a level 5 Diploma, supported by Edinburgh Napier University. Through Code Institute, I have learned to code HTML, CSS, JavaScript (including the use of JQuery) and Python (Using the Django and Flask frameworks). This was so enjoyable, I have taken it upon myself to learn to code in Ruby and React. My real passion is for back end development, but through Code Institute I have learned the skills of a Full Stack Developer.

I am available as a freelance coder (please contact me for more information) and would love to hear from you if you are interested in either recruitment or a collaboration.
